Frequently Asked Questions

  1. Where did the data come from?

    The locations for Michigan were pulled from the the publicly available data on www.michigan.gov

  2. I didn't find any drop boxes based on my location. What gives?

    The list is not 100% comprehensive, and the government's recommendation is to always check with your city's clerk for details. There is also a chance it's a bug in the application and you can always check through the source of the data. (provided above)

  3. What about states other than Michigan?

    I started with my home state, but designed it in a way that I could easily add on as the data becomes available. If you know of the location of data for other states, please email me at fromMeToYute(at)gmail.com and I can take a look into adding them.
